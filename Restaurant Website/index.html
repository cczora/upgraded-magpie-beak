Restaurant Description

Welcome to the best Mom and pop restaurant in the Bay Area!
Pica's Place has been proudly owned and operated by the Swain family since 1972, and we take pride in supplying savory flavors to all our welcomed guests! We offer juicy burgers, smoked brisket, and southern style dishes. All our dishes are served with the best sauces and sides, available, including our famous thick-cut French fries!
